Social action project created by students of the Adolfo Ibáñez University, whose main mission is to generate awareness about the different social problems that afflict Chile as a country, provide support to communities at risk through community building and development projects and mobilize young people students so that as professionals they dedicate themselves to creating a positive impact from their careers and areas of interest. Some of the social projects that the organization generates: construction of emergency housing, social headquarters, playgrounds, workshops for children and adults of the community in vulnerable places, sharing with people in street situations, among others.
